# Fixlt: Product Overview

## 1. Executive Summary

### The Problem
Finding reliable help for small, everyday tasks (assembling furniture, mounting TVs, local moving) is currently a high-friction process. Traditional contracting companies are too expensive or won't take "micro-gigs." Users are forced to rely on inefficient word-of-mouth or unorganized social media groups, lacking a centralized, trustworthy platform.

### The Solution: Fixlt
Fixlt is a cross-platform digital marketplace (mobile app and responsive web platform) connecting:
1. **Requesters:** People looking for help with casual maintenance, repairs, or small physical jobs.
2. **Fixers:** Handy individuals (certified professionals and uncertified locals) willing to perform tasks for payment.

Fixlt creates an efficient, transparent, and accessible digital market with location-based matching, an open bidding system, and real-time communication.

---

## 2. Core User Personas & Features (MVP Scope)

### 2.1 The Requester (The Client)
The user who needs a job done.
* **Task Creation:** Posts detailed requests with title, description, photos, category (Electricity, Plumbing, Carpentry, Painting, Moving, General), budget/suggested price, and location.
* **Location Privacy:** General location pin is public for discovery; exact address is strictly hidden until a bid is accepted.
* **Bid Management:** Receives custom price quotes, reviews Fixer profiles/portfolios/certifications, and accepts the preferred bid.
* **Tracking & Chat:** Tracks task status and communicates via in-app real-time chat.
* **Reviews:** Rates the Fixer (1-5 stars) and leaves a written review upon task completion.

### 2.2 The Fixer (Service Provider)
The user providing the service to earn money.
* **Discovery Feed:** Views open requests in their area via Map or List view.
* **Filtering:** Filters tasks by distance, category, and price range.
* **Bidding System:** Submits inquiries containing a customized Price Offer and a Bid Description/Message.
* **Professional Profile:** Builds reputation via completed jobs, ratings, and reviews. Can upload visual portfolios and professional certifications.
* **Reviews:** Rates the Requester after a job to ensure community safety.

### 2.3 Shared System Features
* **Bilingual Support:** English (LTR) and Hebrew (RTL) toggle.
* **Unified Account System:** Single account to seamlessly toggle between "Requester Mode" and "Fixer Mode".
* **Two-Way Review System:** Mutual rating ecosystem holding both parties accountable.
* **Trust & Safety:** "Email Verified" badge displayed on profiles. Phone number is collected for contact purposes but not verified in the MVP.
* **Real-Time Chat:** In-app messaging for logistics coordination.
* **Notifications:** Push notifications and web alerts for bids, messages, and updates.

## 3. Future Roadmap (Post-MVP)
* **AI Smart Categorization:** Image-recognition (OpenAI/Gemini) to suggest task categories from photos.
* **Live Tracking:** "Fixer is on the Way" live GPS tracking (like Wolt/Uber).
* **Smart Recommendations & Direct Invites:** Algorithmically highlighting tasks for Fixers and allowing Requesters to send direct invites to top-rated Fixers.
* **Admin Dashboard:** Web interface for moderation, dispute resolution, and official verification of credentials.
